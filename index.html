BBB Complaints — Special Rules

⚠️
BBB has legal oversight — treat every complaint seriously

BBB managers manually review all cases. Always issue the refund. Always confirm refund + cancellation in your reply. Never argue. Show we are honest and transparent.

ChatGPT prompt for BBB replies

You are responding on behalf of a customer support team for an online learning platform. A user has left feedback (positive, negative, or neutral). Write a professional and empathetic reply in the same language as the original message. If positive, express appreciation. If negative, apologize sincerely, offer to help, and invite the user to contact support@coursiv.io. Sign off: "Best regards, Coursiv Customer Support Team."

Content complaints → say feedback has been passed to the product team.
Unauthorized charge → explain the platform operates on a subscription basis, disclosed at registration.
Upsell charge → explain the charge was for optional additional content, with fee explained before purchase.
Substream 03

Mentions & Sentiment

YouScan is our social listening backbone. Bad data here means wrong decisions everywhere — keeping it healthy is a core responsibility.

🔴
Bad YouScan setup = bad data = wrong conclusions

If a channel isn't connected or has stopped syncing, that product's mentions won't appear in dashboards. Always verify before reporting.

How YouScan Works

MethodHow It Works
Keyword trackingScans the web for brand/product names and pulls any public mention it finds automatically
Channel connectionYou manually link owned pages, accounts, and stores — YouScan collects everything from them even without a name mention

What Must Be Connected (Per Product)

✅
Connect all of the following for every product

App Store listing · Google Play listing · Trustpilot · Sitejabber · BBB · Reviews.io · HelloPeter · All Facebook ad accounts · Facebook brand pages · Instagram accounts · YouTube channel · Reddit (subreddits + brand mentions)

⚠️
When a new ad account, page, or platform is created for any product

Connect it to YouScan immediately. Waiting even a few days means missing mentions.

Weekly Health Check (Every Monday)

Channels can silently stop syncing. Run this at the start of every week.

  • 1
    Open YouScan
  • 2
    Go to Topics → select the product
  • 3
    Go to Settings → Channels
  • 4
    Check the circular arrow icon next to each channelGrey = working normally · Yellow = data stopped, fix immediately
🚨
If any channel shows yellow

Check all connections and expired authorizations → Contact YouScan via in-app support chat (they respond quickly) → Do NOT fill in sentiment data for that product until fixed.

Tagging Negative Mentions

🏷️
The one rule that must always be followed

Every negative mention must be tagged as either Refund or Other. No exceptions. This is what makes the Refund proportion in the dashboard accurate. Kaisar from payments regularly uses this data — bad tagging misleads product and finance decisions.

Tagging MethodWhen to Use
Keyword-based rulesSet up once — auto-tags mentions containing defined keywords. Best for high-volume recurring complaints.
Manual taggingFor mentions that don't match any rule, or to correct a wrong auto-tag.
💡
One mention can have more than one tag

A complaint about both a refund and a bug gets both the Refund and Bug tags. The dashboard total count will appear higher than reality — that's expected. The proportions are still correct.

Substream 05

Reddit Management

Reddit is one of our most complex and highest-impact reputation channels. This guide explains the strategy, what works, and exactly what to avoid.

🔴
Key insight: You cannot remove Reddit from Google

Once a Reddit thread is indexed for your brand search, there's no way to delete it from Google results, suppress it with SEO alone, or contact Reddit/Google to remove it. The only effective strategy is to outcompete it — create positive content with more engagement so Google ranks that higher instead.

Why Reddit Matters

700k+
Users/month who see our ads then search our brand
40.1%
Of LLM citations come from Reddit (Semrush 2025)
#1
Source cited by AI models

Negative Reddit threads directly reduce conversion rates. They've also affected our ability to work with media partners and raised concerns with banks and compliance teams.

What We Do on Reddit

When and How to Reply from the Official Account

⚠️
Do not reply to every negative comment

Some users have publicly complained that the brand "sneaks around in the comments section." Replying too often draws more attention and looks defensive or coordinated. Only reply when it genuinely reduces risk.

Reply when:

  • →
    User is highly vocal and mentions authorities, BBB, police, or chargebacks
  • →
    Complaint is specific and factual — a public reply adds credibility

How to reply:

  • 1
    Write a calm, professional public reply acknowledging the issue
  • 2
    Ask user to DM you their email to resolve privately
  • 3
    Resolve in private — refund, cancel, explain the charge

What Gets Posts Indexed on Google

💡
Most important insight from 9 months of testing

Comment and upvote count alone does NOT determine ranking. A well-optimised post in a big subreddit can index in 2 days with minimal engagement. A poorly written post in a small subreddit may never index regardless of upvotes.

FactorWhy It Matters
Keyword-dense SEO titleGoogle reads the title to understand relevance
Long, well-written post bodySignals quality to Google's crawler
Large, active subreddit (1M+ members ideal)High-authority source = faster and higher ranking
High-karma account postingCredibility signal with Reddit's algorithm
Early engagement in first 24hGoogle evaluates early traction to rank the post

The Engagement Formula

  • 1
    Publish from a high-karma account in a large subreddit
  • 2
    First 24h: add comments and upvotes via freelancerSignals early traction to Google and Reddit
  • 3
    Share via search ads and socialDrives additional traffic to boost engagement signal
  • 4
    Continue adding comments and upvotes weeklyMaintains ranking, prevents being overtaken again
  • 5
    Add backlinks from external sourcesStrengthens domain authority signal for Google

What NOT to Do

🚫
These actions will get you banned or create new damage

Have office employees comment from the office — shared office IP = all accounts get blocked.
Use AI-generated text for comments — Reddit's spam detection is sophisticated.
Send users from a single in-app link to comment — Reddit traces referral patterns, post gets removed.
Try to bribe subreddit moderators — if they publish it, you create a new thread with far more exposure.

What performs best:

  • ★
    AI-related memesHighest engagement by far
  • ★
    Reposts of popular content from bigger communitiesLow effort, proven content = reliable engagement
  • ★
    Reposts of our content back into bigger communitiesFor reach
⚠️
Account authenticity rules — critical

Spend 2–3 hours/day on Reddit browsing unrelated posts. Always use a US VPN and private browser on a dedicated device. The account must look like a real user, not a brand account.
